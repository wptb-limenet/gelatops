Managing a gelateria without the right tools means...

🧮

Recipes in scattered spreadsheets

Version chaos, broken formulas, no cost visibility. Which tab is the latest version of Stracciatella?

📋

Shopping lists made by hand

Recalculated every production run, prone to errors. One miscalculation means a mid-run trip back to the supplier.

💸

No real-time food cost tracking

You don't know your margin until it's too late. Prices change; your spreadsheet doesn't update itself.

⚠️

Allergen compliance done manually

One missed ingredient can be a legal and safety risk. EU food labelling law doesn't forgive spreadsheet oversights.

📦

Orders disconnected from production

Customer orders never automatically feed your production plan. More copying, more errors, more time lost.

🏷️

Labels printed from Word files

Time-consuming and not automatically updated when recipes change. A recipe tweak means a new label design session.

Everything your gelateria needs

One platform. All the tools.

🍦

Recipe Management

Build and manage all your ice cream and sorbet recipes. Set ingredient amounts per litre, track which recipes use which ingredients, and keep everything versioned and organised.

💰

Real-Time Food Cost

See the cost of every recipe at 1L, 5L, 10L and 15L instantly. Update ingredient prices once and every recipe recalculates automatically. Know your margins before you produce.

📋

Production Planning

Build production plans manually or let incoming orders populate them automatically. Submit a plan and get a frozen shopping list with prices locked at that moment in time.

🛒

Smart Shopping Lists

Aggregate ingredient requirements across your entire production plan in one click. Export to CSV or print. Know exactly how much of everything to buy before you go to market.

⚠️

Allergen Tracking

Tag ingredients with EU allergens once. Every recipe automatically inherits the correct allergen list. Stay compliant with food labelling regulations without extra manual work.

🔗

Order Integration

Connect your online shop via Stripe webhooks. Customer orders automatically land in your production queue with the right recipe IDs. No double entry, no missed orders.

⚗️

Smart Recipe Balancing

Automatically adjust ingredient ratios within seconds to achieve the perfect texture at serving temperature — for both gelato and sorbets. Fine-tune sweetness levels on the fly while the system preserves the texture profile you've dialled in.
